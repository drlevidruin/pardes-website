Middle School at Pardes

Rigor does not mean harder. It means smarter.

Blended groups, project-based learning, and self-directed growth. Every student challenged and supported.

Rigorous. Applied. Real.

Math, science, ELA, and social studies taught through blended instructional groups and applied, project-based learning.

Students are assessed with NWEA MAP Growth twice a year. Each student gets a laptop and learns to use technology wisely, including age-appropriate AI literacy.

Every student is challenged at the right level.

Torah. Chumash. Identity.

Torah, Chumash, Hebrew language, Gemara, and Jewish philosophy rooted in Chabad values.

Students daven together each morning and explore ethical questions through deep discussion. Jewish identity at Pardes is something students appreciate and make their own.

Judaism is not a subject. It is how we live.

Real-world experience,
built in.

Every student completes at least one semester of real-world internship during their middle school journey. Real work, real responsibility, real growth.

Study skills, time management, and academic advising prepare students for the transition to high school. Testing results can be forwarded to high schools upon request.

Pardes graduates enter high school ready to lead.
