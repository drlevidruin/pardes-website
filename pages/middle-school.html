Practical Details

What families need to know.

School Hours

Monday-Thursday: 8:10 AM - 4:00 PM

Friday: 8:10 AM - 2:00 PM

Drop-off: 7:50 - 8:10 AM

Location: 7055 Bonita Dr, Miami Beach, FL 33141

Meals & Nutrition

A breakfast bar is open every morning with fresh fruits, vegetables, yogurts, granola, and other healthy options. Students bring lunch from home or enjoy Pizza Fridays.

All food at school is cholov yisroel and pas yisroel. No candy, gum, or soda.

Uniform

Pardes branded polo shirts (short or long sleeve), skirts or dresses for girls, shorts or pants for boys, and Pardes sweatshirts. Sneakers are recommended daily and required on PE days.

Technology

Each student is assigned a laptop for the school year with advanced filtering software for safety and focus. Students learn to balance mindful tech use with meaningful offline experiences.
