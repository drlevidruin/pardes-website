Rigorous & Relevant

Rigor doesn't mean harder. It means smarter.

Precision education. Intentional instruction. Every student challenged and supported.

Core Academics

Advanced coursework in math, science, English language arts, and social studies aligned to JSAT standards. Fluid instructional groups and multiple teachers working simultaneously ensure every student gets the precision support they need to excel.

High School Readiness

Study skills workshops, time management coaching, and academic advising prepare students for the transition to high school with confidence and independence.

Technology & Innovation

Hands-on learning with robotics, coding, and AI literacy. Students develop the technical fluency needed for tomorrow's world while building critical thinking skills.

Jewish Identity

Deep engagement with Torah study, Hebrew language, and Jewish philosophy. Students explore ethical questions and discover what it means to connect with their heritage on their own level. At Pardes, Jewish identity is something students appreciate and make their own.

Beyond the Classroom

The Pardes Way in action.

Leadership, service, real-life skills, and authentic voice. The years that shape who students become.

Leadership & Service

Student government, community service projects, and mentorship programs help students develop responsibility, empathy, and the skills to lead.

Arts & Expression

Music, visual arts, drama, and creative writing give students space to explore their voice and express themselves with confidence.

Sports & Wellness

Competitive and recreational athletics, mindfulness practices, and real-life skills development support the whole student. body, mind, and spirit.

Clubs & Enrichment

From robotics and debate to Judaic clubs and cooking, our after-school programs let students pursue passions and discover new interests.
