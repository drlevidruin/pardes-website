Academics

Skills and content, working together.

Precision education. Every child advances.

Our elementary approach is intentional from the start. Mornings focus on core literacy and math when minds are fresh. We use our proprietary English curriculum and HMH Into Math, paired with NWEA MAP Growth monitoring to ensure every child progresses. Afternoons bring those skills to life through science, social studies, and real-world projects that make learning meaningful.

Multiple teachers working simultaneously in every classroom means we respond to each learner's pace and needs by design. Students develop mastery and agency. They experience learning as joyful and purposeful.

Judaics

Practical, joyful Jewish education.

Rooted in Chabad tradition, connected to real life.

Our Judaics program teaches Hebrew language and Jewish texts while grounding learning in values and purpose. Students don't study Judaism from a distance - they see how Torah wisdom, mitzvot, and tradition guide daily decisions, build relationships, and strengthen community.

Through holiday celebrations, service learning, and thoughtful discussion, students develop a living connection to their heritage. They leave elementary school with both knowledge and identity - understanding not just what Judaism teaches, but why it matters to who they are.

Real-Life Skills

Character woven into everything.

Collaboration, emotional management, and resilience become part of who they are.

Real-life skills are not a separate program at Pardes. they are embedded in how we teach, how we greet each other, and how we resolve challenges. Through our LeHashlim framework, students practice emotional awareness, listening deeply, and making responsible choices in real time, every day.

Our teachers are skilled coaches. They notice when a student is struggling, they model respectful communication, and they help children build confidence and empathy through consistent guidance. The result is a classroom culture where every child feels capable of growth.

Enrichment & Future Ready

Preparing minds for tomorrow.

Technology and innovation, age-appropriately.

We introduce technology purposefully, ensuring it enhances learning rather than replaces thinking. Starting in 3rd grade, students explore foundational technology concepts, including age-appropriate AI literacy and forward-thinking tools like Waggle mathematics, with thoughtful expansion over time.

The goal is not early screen time - it's building a generation of students who understand how to use tools wisely, think critically about technology, and bring creativity and problem-solving to challenges. Pardes graduates are ready for anything.
