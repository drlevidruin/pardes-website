Common Questions

What families ask us.

What grades does Pardes Day School serve?

We serve students from Preschool (Gan Katan, 18 months) through 8th grade. Our three divisions each have their own leadership team while sharing one educational philosophy.

What is your application timeline?

Applications are accepted on a rolling basis. We recommend starting the process in the fall for the following school year. Early submissions receive priority consideration.

Do you offer financial aid or scholarships?

Yes. We offer need-based financial aid and accept all major Florida scholarship programs. Contact our office at (305) 690-2548 to discuss options for your family.

How does Pardes support different learning needs?

With multiple teachers in every classroom and fluid instructional groups, students who learn differently receive direct, targeted support every day. This is not an add-on. It is the normal rhythm of school.
