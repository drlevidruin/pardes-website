Getting Started

Our four-step admissions process.

Transparent, welcoming, and designed to support every family.

1

Schedule a Tour

Visit our campus and experience Pardes firsthand. Meet faculty, explore classrooms, and feel the community energy.

2

Submit Application

Complete our streamlined application with your family's information. We're here to support you through every step.

3

Family Interview

We'll schedule a conversation to learn about your educational values and goals for your child's growth.

4

Welcome to Pardes

You'll hear from our admissions team with next steps. We're excited to welcome your family to our community.
