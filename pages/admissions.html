Getting Started

Our four-step admissions process.

Transparent, welcoming, and designed to support every family.

1

Inquire

Fill out our inquiry form below. Our office will reach out to schedule a campus tour at a time that works for your family.

2

Tour Our Campus

Visit Pardes in action. Walk the classrooms, meet faculty, and see The Pardes Way for yourself. Tours are scheduled individually so we can give your family our full attention.

3

Parent Conversation

We interview parents, not children. We believe the strongest partnerships start with understanding how families and school can grow together. Parents are welcome to bring their children to see the school.

4

Welcome to the Family

Once we know we are the right fit for each other, we will walk you through enrollment and onboarding so your family feels at home from day one.
