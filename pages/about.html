Our Story

It started with five children. It kept growing because it felt right.

In 2011, Katy Horowitz moved from London to Miami Beach with a simple belief: school can be joyful, serious, and deeply personal. She started with five children in a living room.

That small beginning became Gan Katan, then a kindergarten in 2017, then a grade added every year. Families kept coming because the school felt warm, close, and full of life.

Today, Pardes serves more than 220 students across two campuses from preschool through 8th grade. It is bigger now, but it still feels like a place where children are known well and families feel at home.
