Independence

Built by design, with warmth.

Children flourish in an environment built by design with warmth and intention. We help each child develop independence: caring for their belongings, using language to express needs, and participating proudly in shared routines.

Our teachers provide the structure and genuine connection that make children feel confident and cared for.

Jewish Life

Jewish Life

Lived and practical, not distant.

Through songs, blessings, celebrations, and everyday mitzvot, Jewish life is lived and practical, not distant or abstract.

Children discover that being Jewish means caring for others, marking time with joy, and building community where everyone belongs.

Our Classrooms

Small classes. Big attention.

11 to 20 students per class from our youngest learners to our oldest, so every child gets the attention they deserve.

3 to 4 teachers in every classroom. Not one adult managing a crowd. A team of educators working together, every day.

Four classrooms by age: Daisies (turning 2), Sunflowers (turning 3), Caterpillars (turning 4), and Butterflies (turning 5 / PreK). Each class has its own rhythm.
