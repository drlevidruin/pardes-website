# Pardes Website Design Rules

Hard rules from the owner. Apply to ALL pages.

## Copy Rules
- NO em dashes (-, -) ever. Use periods or commas.
- No cliche descriptor lines under heroes
- Headlines must not have orphaned words on their own line
- Each sentence in multi-line CTAs goes on its own line
- Button labels must match exactly what happens when clicked
- "2+" teachers per classroom (not "3-4", not every class has that many)
- Check real site data before writing any facts or numbers

## Visual Rules
- Caveat handwritten font ONLY on section labels (max 1 per section, never overused)
- Green bold `<strong>` for inline highlights (NOT pill/badge tags)
- White space: keep it tight. Owner prefers less padding, not more.
- Body text minimum 16px, minimum color #424245
- White background default. Sage (#f0f4ee) for ONE key section per page max.
- Dark forest green (#0a4a2b) for CTA bands

## Brand Rules
- School is FULL. Site is brand showcase, not admissions funnel.
- "Meet the Team" in header (not "Schedule a Tour")
- "Get in Touch" for bottom page CTAs (not "Schedule a Tour")
- The Pardes Way is the core differentiator. Never lose the message in the design.
- Warm and inviting, not cold and corporate

## Process Rules
- ALWAYS mockup everything before pushing to live site
- Owner reviews and approves mockups on real phone before implementation
- Owner makes all editorial decisions (AI proposes, owner decides)
